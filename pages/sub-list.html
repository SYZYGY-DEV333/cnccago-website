Substitute Organists

The AGO maintains a list of credible organists within the chapter who are willing to serve as substitutes in the area.
These are organized according to three main classifications:
  • S - Available to work on Sundays
  • D - Qualified to direct choirs
  • O - Available to work on other occasions throughout the week, such as Weddings and Funerals.
